# Tomato___Late_blight
Symptoms: Dark, water-soaked lesions on leaves and stems; white fungal growth under humid conditions.
Actions:
- Remove severely infected leaves; avoid overhead irrigation.
- Apply fungicide with active ingredients like chlorothalonil or copper (follow local guidelines).
- Ensure good airflow; rotate crops; disinfect tools.

# Potato___Early_blight
Symptoms: Brown concentric ring spots on older leaves.
Actions:
- Remove debris; improve soil nutrition.
- Use labeled fungicides as per extension guidelines.
- Practice crop rotation and mulching to reduce splash.

# Healthy
Actions:
- No treatment required. Recommend monitoring and good cultural practices.

# Tomato___Early_blight
Symptoms: Brown concentric rings on older leaves; yellowing around lesions.
Actions:
- Remove infected foliage and plant debris.
- Use labeled fungicides; rotate modes of action.
- Improve airflow; avoid overhead irrigation.

# Tomato___Leaf_Mold
Symptoms: Olive-green to gray mold on underside of leaves; yellow patches on top.
Actions:
- Increase ventilation and reduce humidity.
- Remove infected leaves; sanitize tools.
- Apply appropriate fungicides if necessary.

# Potato___Late_blight
Symptoms: Water-soaked lesions on leaves; white mold under humid conditions; tuber rot.
Actions:
- Remove infected plants; avoid overhead watering.
- Use fungicides per extension guidelines.
- Rotate crops; destroy cull piles.
